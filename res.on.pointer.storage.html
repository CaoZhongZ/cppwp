20.5.5.13 Restrictions on storage of pointers [res.on.pointer.storage]

#

Objects constructed by the standard library that may hold a user-supplied pointer value or an integer of type std​::​intptr_­t shall store such values in a traceable pointer location. [ Note: Other libraries are strongly encouraged to do the same, since not doing so may result in accidental use of pointers that are not safely derived.
