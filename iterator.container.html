24 Iterators library [iterators]

24.8 Container access [iterator.container]

1
#

In addition to being available via inclusion of the <iterator> header, the function templates in [iterator.container] are available when any of the following headers are included: <array>, <deque>, <forward_list>, <list>, <map>, <regex>, <set>, <string>, <unordered_map>, <unordered_set>, and <vector>.
