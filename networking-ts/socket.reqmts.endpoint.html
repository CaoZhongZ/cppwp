18.2.4 Endpoint requirements [socket.reqmts.endpoint]

1

A type X meets the Endpoint requirements if it satisfies the requirements of Destructible (destructible), DefaultConstructible (defaultconstructible), CopyConstructible (copyconstructible), and CopyAssignable (copyassignable), as well as the additional requirements listed below.
