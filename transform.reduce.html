3
#
Requires:
  • (3.1)
    T shall be Cpp17MoveConstructible (Table 26).
  • (3.2)
    All of
    • (3.2.1)
      binary_­op1(init, init),
    • (3.2.2)
      binary_­op1(init, binary_­op2(*first1, *first2)),
    • (3.2.3)
      binary_­op1(binary_­op2(*first1, *first2), init), and
    • (3.2.4)
      binary_­op1(binary_­op2(*first1, *first2), binary_­op2(*first1, *first2))
    shall be convertible to T.
  • (3.3)
    Neither binary_­op1 nor binary_­op2 shall invalidate subranges, nor modify elements in the ranges [first1, last1] and [first2, first2 + (last1 - first1)].
