  • (2.1)

  • (2.2)

  • (2.3)

3

4
#
Effects:
  • (4.1)
    If I and S model Assignable<I&, S>, equivalent to i = std::move(bound).
  • (4.2)
    Otherwise, if S and I model SizedSentinel<S, I>, equivalent to ranges::advance(i, bound - i).
  • (4.3)
    Otherwise, while bool(i != bound) is true, increments i.
